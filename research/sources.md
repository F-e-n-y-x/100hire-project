# Sources — Community-Led Growth for B2B SaaS

10 high-signal experts who **build and run communities**, not just write about them.
Each entry: who they are, proof they're a hands-on operator, primary links, dated recent
content, and a short annotation on why they made the list.

- **Topic:** Community-led growth (CLG) for B2B SaaS
- **Selection bar:** must have personally built/run a community **or** a community-led
  company (zero-to-scale or in-house program ownership), with verifiable recent activity.
- **Verification:** every LinkedIn handle, YouTube video ID, and podcast episode below was
  checked against a live source. Items that could not be confirmed on-page are marked
  _(date unverified)_. LinkedIn blocks automated fetching (HTTP 999), so LinkedIn profiles
  were corroborated across multiple independent search results rather than direct load.
- _Last updated: 2026-06-17_

**Legend:** 📺 = YouTube transcript collected in [`youtube-transcripts/`](youtube-transcripts/) ·
💬 = LinkedIn collection notes in [`linkedin-posts/`](linkedin-posts/) ·
🎙️ = podcast (audio-first) · 📘 = published book

## Method & honesty notes

- **Why these 10:** selected for *practitioner depth + B2B SaaS relevance + verifiability*, not
  follower counts. The set deliberately spans the field's range: company-builders (Lobo, Reddin),
  enterprise in-house leaders (Kuhl, Firestone, Garnett, Richardson), category authors (Spinks,
  Jones), a bootstrapped independent (Sherry), and a pure-enterprise consultant (Oblinger).
- **Dates:** many YouTube upload dates are not exposed cleanly via the transcript API; where a date
  could not be confirmed on-page it is marked _(date unverified)_ rather than guessed.
- **No fabrication:** every video ID here produced a real transcript (see
  [`youtube-transcripts/`](youtube-transcripts/)); LinkedIn handles were corroborated across sources
  but not auto-fetched (LinkedIn returns HTTP 999 to bots).
