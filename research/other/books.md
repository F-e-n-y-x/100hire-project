# Books by the Experts

The category's foundational texts — useful as primary references when turning this research
into a playbook.

| Author | Book | Year | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lloyed Lobo | _From Grassroots to Greatness: 13 Rules to Build Iconic Brands with Community-Led Growth_ | 2023 | WSJ/Amazon bestseller; the most directly on-topic CLG playbook. https://www.lloyedlobo.com/ |
| David Spinks | _The Business of Belonging: How to Make Community Your Competitive Advantage_ | 2021 | Category-defining book on community-as-strategy. |
| Carrie Melissa Jones (with Charles Vogl) | _Building Brand Communities: How Organizations Succeed by Creating Belonging_ | 2020 | Berrett-Koehler; most-cited practitioner book on brand community. |
| Bailey Richardson (with Kevin Huynh & Kai Elmer Sotto) | _Get Together: How to Build a Community With Your People_ | 2019 | Stripe Press; "Purposeful, Participatory, Repeatable" framework. |
| Christina Garnett | _Transforming Customer–Brand Relationships_ | 2025 | Kogan Page; most recent of the set. https://www.koganpage.com/marketing-communications/transforming-customer-brand-relationships-9781398621329 |
