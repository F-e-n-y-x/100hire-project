## Post — Easter-egg engagement campaign case study (OpenSpace)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ericakuhl_community-engagement-is-something-that-takes-activity-6928001116932644865-gln2
- Engagement: 121 reactions, 19 comments

> Community engagement is something that takes nonstop attention and creativity. Nikiya Crisostomo,
> P.E. from OpenSpace... came up with a brilliant idea to try to boost engagement in her community.
> The Engagement Idea: hid creatively designed easter eggs all around OpenSpace properties... Once
> customers spotted an Easter Egg they needed to take a screenshot... Every new Easter Egg they found
> counted as a raffle entry... The Results: All-time 3rd highest weekly # visitors [70% above avg];
> All-time 2nd highest weekly # guest visitors [75% above avg]; All-time highest weekly # page views
> [185% & 240% above avg]. Summary: community leader + creative idea + clear goals = amazing
> engagement boost!
